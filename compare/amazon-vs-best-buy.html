Amazon vs Best Buy: Which Is Actually Cheaper? (2026)

We tracked 200 products across both retailers for 60 days. The results reveal a clear winner — but it depends entirely on what you're buying.

📅 March 2026⏱ 7 min read✓ Data-backed
Short answer: Amazon wins on smart home devices, books, and household items. Best Buy wins on laptops, TVs, and open-box electronics. For phones and headphones, they're essentially tied — whoever has a sale that week wins.

How We Compared Amazon vs Best Buy

From January through March 2026, CloudDeals tracked the prices of 200 products across Amazon and Best Buy. We recorded prices every 6 hours and calculated the average, minimum, and maximum price for each product at each retailer over the 60-day period.

This isn't a single-day snapshot — it's 60 days of continuous data that shows which retailer is actually cheaper over time, not just on one particular day.

When Best Buy Beats Amazon

Best Buy has one weapon Amazon can't easily match: open-box inventory. Items returned in like-new or excellent condition get marked down 10–30% and are available in-store and online. For laptops and TVs especially, this creates genuine deals that Amazon's marketplace can't replicate at scale.

Best Buy also has a price match guarantee. If you find a lower price at Amazon (or any major retailer) within 15 days of your Best Buy purchase, they'll refund the difference. This means you can sometimes buy confidently from Best Buy knowing you're protected if Amazon drops the price.

When Amazon Beats Best Buy

Amazon's real advantage is in its own hardware ecosystem. Echo devices, Fire tablets, and Kindle e-readers are priced below what Best Buy can match because Amazon subsidizes them to drive Prime and Alexa usage. The Echo Dot regularly sits 15–25% below Best Buy's price for the same unit.

Amazon also wins through Prime exclusives — flash deals available only to Prime members that last a few hours. Best Buy can't participate in these. CloudDeals monitors these and sends alerts when they hit.

Best Buy's Open-Box: The Hidden Edge

In our 60-day study, Best Buy's open-box section averaged 21% below new Amazon pricing for laptops and TVs. Open-box "excellent" condition items are essentially new — they were returned, inspected, and repackaged.

The catch: availability is unpredictable and inventory is location-specific. You can't always find the exact model you want in open-box. But when it's available, it's often the best deal in the market.

Bottom Line: Use Both

The smartest strategy isn't picking one retailer — it's tracking prices at both and buying from whoever is cheaper when you're ready. That's exactly what CloudDeals does automatically.

  • For laptops and TVs: Check Best Buy open-box first, then compare
