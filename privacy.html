Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026 · Cloud Design Studio LLC

Cloud Design Studio LLC ("CloudDeals," "we," "our," or "us") operates CloudDeals.com and the CloudDeals iOS application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly: When you join our waitlist or sign up for deal alerts, we collect your email address. We do not require you to create an account to browse the website.

Information collected automatically: When you visit CloudDeals.com, we may automatically collect certain information including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, and time spent on pages. This information is collected through standard web analytics tools (Google Analytics 4).

Mobile app information: When you use the CloudDeals iOS app, we collect information necessary to provide the service, including your device identifier, push notification token (to send price alerts), and watchlist data (products you track and target prices). This data is stored securely in our Firebase backend.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To send you deal alerts and price drop notifications you have requested
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the CloudDeals website and iOS app
  • To send weekly deal digest emails (only if you have subscribed)
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our content and features
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Amazon Associates Disclosure

CloudDeals is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com. Our Amazon Associate Store ID is clouddeals20-20.

When you click an Amazon product link on CloudDeals.com or through the CloudDeals app and make a purchase, we earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. This is how we fund the free tier of our service.

We also participate in affiliate programs with eBay Partner Network and Walmart Affiliate Program. Similar commission structures apply.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. These include:

  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics 4): Help us understand how visitors interact with our site
  • Essential cookies: Required for basic site functionality
  • Affiliate tracking cookies: Set by Amazon and other affiliate partners when you click product links

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Service providers: Firebase (Google) for app backend, Google Analytics for web analytics
  • Affiliate partners: Amazon, eBay, and Walmart receive standard affiliate tracking data when you click product links
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

6. Data Retention

We retain your email address for as long as you are subscribed to our communications. You may unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send. App account data is retained until you delete your account.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time
  • Opt out of analytics tracking (via browser settings or Google Analytics opt-out)

8. Children's Privacy

CloudDeals is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. App data is stored in Google Firebase with industry-standard security. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.
