When Do Echo Devices Go on Sale?

Based on our price tracking data, Amazon Echo devices see their biggest discounts during three windows:

  • Prime Day (July) — typically 30–40% off. This is when Amazon pushes their own hardware hardest.
  • Black Friday / Cyber Monday (November) — all-time lows happen here more often than any other sale. The Echo Dot hit $29.99 on Black Friday 2025.
  • Amazon's Spring Sale (March–April) — a smaller sale event, usually 15–25% off. We're in this window now.

Amazon Echo vs. Google Nest: Which Is Worth It?

If you're in the Amazon ecosystem (Prime, Alexa skills, Ring cameras, Fire TV), Echo is the clear winner. The integration is seamless. If you use Google services (Google Calendar, Gmail, Chromecast), a Nest speaker makes more sense.

For price comparison: a comparable Google Nest Audio runs $99.99 — the same as the Echo 4th Gen. At the entry level, the Echo Dot at $49.99 beats the Google Nest Mini at $49.99 on audio quality by a noticeable margin in our testing.
